Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about earning with HYPEHIVE

How much money can I earn? +
Earnings vary based on your activity level. Most users earn $20-50 per month, while active premium members can earn $100+ monthly. You earn $0.02-0.10 per video watched and $0.50-2.00 when others watch your videos.
How do I withdraw my earnings? +
Once you reach the $5 minimum, you can withdraw to PayPal, direct bank transfer, cryptocurrency wallets, or gift cards. Withdrawals are processed instantly for PayPal and within 1-2 business days for bank transfers.
What kind of videos will I watch? +
Our admin team curates a variety of high-quality video ads from reputable brands and businesses. Videos include product demos, service advertisements, promotional content, and educational material. All content is family-friendly and safe.
Is HYPEHIVE free to join? +
Yes! Creating an account and starting to earn is completely free. Our optional Premium subscription ($9.99/month) offers additional benefits like higher payouts and exclusive content, but it's not required to earn money.
Can I post any type of video content? +
Videos must be appropriate, legal, and comply with our content guidelines. We accept promotional videos, product demonstrations, service advertisements, educational content, and entertainment videos. All submissions are reviewed before going live.
